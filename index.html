About Us

Welcome to The Cake Shop, Cakes Shine.The Cake Shop is a sweet family owned bakery located in Perinthalmanna, Malappuram. We pride ourselves with delivering the sweetest cakes to you! All of our cakes are made fresh from scratch with every order. We carefully select our ingredients for their freshness, flavor and natural value. We choose organic, locally grown products when available. We are grateful to our amazing staff, loyal customers, and the most supportive local community who allow us to do the work we love every day.We promise to leave you satisfied and full of happiness with every bite!
